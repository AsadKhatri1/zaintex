About Zaintex

Zaintex is one of Buying & Sourcing house for Textiles & Textile made ups from Pakistan . Our aim is to mediate between Exporters of Pakistan & Importers from around the Globe to facilitate them with the best of our efforts & Skills.

Our Philosophy is to bring on Forefront exporters of this region to meet genuine buyers of their products, Exporters who have the capability to produce up to the Standards as set forth, Always enjoy privilege of continuos orders with us. Because, We at Zaintex, not only strive to bridge the Gap of communication between buyers & sellers, But we endeavor ourselves in all the aspects of Production as well.

Importers interested in the Cotton Rich market of Pakistan , some times face problems finding suitable suppliers of their desired merchandise & vice versa. Now these pains taking efforts remain no more their concern, with our services & team of dedicated professionals, Importers just have to place an order with us & rest is taken care by our automated Command & Control system.
